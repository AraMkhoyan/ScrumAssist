Daily Scrum Suggestions

Recommendations

We recommend that you have Daily Scrum Meetings every day for 15 minutes. However, you can skip it once in a while for emergency cases. This recommendation is based on your team size, the level of collaboration, and the team’s habits. Here is more information about Daily Scrum and how you should utilize it.

  • For groups 6-10 Use Daily Scrum for 15 mins every day (Diebold, Ostberg, Wagner, & Zendler, 2015)
  • If you think that the team is well aware of their daily tasks and there is no need for quick collaboration, Daily Scrum can be skipped (Prokpets, n.d.)
  • You can skip it if you care a lot about performance and prefer your employees to work (Mersino, 2019)
  • Sometimes when people are confused about what to work on for that day, you can have a Daily Scrum even if it was not scheduled (Kniberg, 2015)
  • You can skip it if not planned to be in the morning, if people are usually being late, or Scrum Master or Product Owner is not usually present (Martín de Agar, J., 2018).
  • Aspects of the Daily Scrum the team can modify
    • The meets should be 30 minutes as they always go over 15 minutes
    • The three main questions from Scrum Guide should be replaced with questions that the developers usually care more about and speak about during the meeting
    • People who are confused about what to work on for the day or have questions should be given the priority to speak
    • There should be other means of communication between developers to keep and encourage collaboration
