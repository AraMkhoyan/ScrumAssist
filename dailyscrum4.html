Daily Scrum Suggestions

Recommendations

We recommend that you run Daily Scrum Meetings on emergency cases (not often). This is mainly because of your current team behavior of not using daily scrum yet being productive. Here are more details on how to use Daily Scrum for your team.

  • If you don’t hold Daily Scrum meetings - Run it on emergency situations (Prokopets, n.d.)
  • Run if people are confused about what to work on for today and make sure that during the meeting you follow the structure and everyone shares their progress and updates (Kniberg, 2015)
  • Daily Scrum is not effective for two teams (not revealed in the study) because…
    • Not all key questions according to Scrum Guide are addressed
    • Not all team members contribute
    • Daily scrum events take longer than 15 minutes
    • No fixed time for the daily scrum event
  • Daily Scrum should be completely skipped for this case since
    • Teammates are not being collaborative during the meeting and the meeting does not serve its purpose
    • The 3 key questions are not being addressed
    • The Product Owner skipped the meeting
    • There is no fixed structure in place for the meetings (e.g., fixed time)
  • When you have some, feel free to modify the following aspects of the Daily Scrum
    • The meets should be 30 minutes as they always go over 15 minutes
    • The three main questions from Scrum Guide should be replaced with questions that the developers usually care more about and speak about during the meeting
    • People who are confused about what to work on for the day or have questions should be given the priority to speak
    • There should be other means of communication between developers to keep and encourage collaboration
